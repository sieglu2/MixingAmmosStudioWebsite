Mixing Ammos

You are Ely, the team leader of the space exploration group "Xipheral," alongside your colleagues Astra and Jennifer.
Tracking the infamous criminal Exo, you find yourself at an uncharted space station absent from the space administration's database.
The story begins...

Engage in Strategic Turn-Based Combat

"Mixing Ammos" brings you a strategic turn-based gameplay experience, rooted in a system reminiscent of rock-paper-scissors.
Each stage tests your endurance with activities like walking, running, and sprinting, which can quickly wear you out.
Master three unique attack methods to dominate your foes:

  • Shooting - Launch rockets to assault enemies from long distances.
  • Sword Aura - Unleash an aura with a sword swing for mid-range attacks.
  • Force Shield - Deploy a protective shield to defend against assaults.

Understanding the Attack Interactions

  • Shooting vs. Force Shield: Force Shield blocks shooting attacks, providing anti-damage protection.
  • Shooting vs. Sword Aura: Shooting attacks harm opponents beyond Sword Auraâ€™s reach.
  • Sword Aura vs. Force Shield: Sword Aura shatters Force Shield, inflicting extra damage.
  • Sword Aura vs. Shooting: Sword Aura deals reduced damage if the enemy is using shooting attacks.
  • Force Shield vs. Shooting: Force Shield blocks shooting attacks and can counter with anti-attack damage.
  • Force Shield vs. Sword Aura: Force Shield sustains more damage when attacked by Sword Aura.
