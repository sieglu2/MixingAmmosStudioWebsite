Shadow Frontlines

Overview

"Shadow Frontlines" is a dynamic game that blends PvE and PvP confrontations with a unique twist on teamwork. Players are divided into two teams, with Team A being the primary team and Team B either controlled by AI or another player.

Team A: The Primary Force

Commander

  • The backbone of Team A.
  • The game ends if the Commander is killed.
  • Commands from an aerial view, deploying drones to scout hidden areas.
  • Provides crucial intelligence to Field Agents, but cannot engage in direct combat.

Field Agents

  • The frontline fighters, similar to players in other FPS games.
  • Act on intelligence from the Commander to strategize and destroy enemy bases.
  • Can respawn at the Commanderâ€™s location after a cooldown period.

Team B: The Adversary

AI-Controlled or Player-Managed:

  • Begins each round by designating respawn areas for bots.
  • Gains points by eliminating Field Agents, which can be used to deploy more bots.
  • Can choose respawn locations for new bots strategically.

Game Modes

"Shadow Frontlines" offers a variety of engaging game modes:

  • Defense: Protect specific areas from enemy attacks.
  • Escort: Safeguard valuable goods from one location to another without allowing any damage.
  • Death Match: Engage in a classic battle to eliminate the opposing team.
  • Destroy Key Points: Target and destroy critical locations.
